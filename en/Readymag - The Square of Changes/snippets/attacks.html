Attacks on “PP”

In late October, the conflict reached a new level. Unknown men and women with their faces coved with masks and hoods started attacking the protest yards. They were well-equipped and had paint for covering the graffiti, and scissors and torches – for destroying the ribbons. They called themselves "not indifferent citizens" or refused to answer the questions of the locals.

Within a short period of time, such groups visited many protest backyards in Minsk: Novaya Brovaya, Borovlyany, Mayak Minsk, Cascade, Grushevka and others. They came to the Square of Changes three times: on October 21 and 30 and November 11. The residents of the Square of Changes tried to stop the vandalism.
